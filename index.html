I am a first-year PhD student at University of British Columbia exploring applications of reinforcement learning to robotics with professor Michiel Van de Panne. I have a wide range of interests but my current research focus is mostly on representation learning applied to reinforcement learning problems.

Before, I was a reinforcement learning researcher at Wayve, applying machine learning to robotics control to teach cars how to drive themselves.

I completed my B.Sc. at Polytechnic University of Turin and my M.Sc jointly between Polytechnic University of Turin and Telecom ParisTech - Eurecom Research Center. I was a visiting researcher at Berkeley AI Research at UC Berkeley for 8 months where I did research for my master thesis with professor Ruzena Bajcsy related to graphical models and statistical learning.

Sometimes I write about random stuff over here.
